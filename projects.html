Project Ignition

Fuel is valuable. You're not. In this game, your fuel is your weapon. Drop pools of flammable gasoline behind you, and then run back over it to set it ablaze! Be sure to keep an eye on your gas gauge and find a gas pump when it gets too low. With a Mad Max inspired theme and Tron-like game-play, driving through a scarred wasteland has never been more fun.
